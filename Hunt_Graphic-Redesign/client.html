Our Past Clients

Racerman Racing

Back in 2003, Hunt Graphic worked with Racerman Racing to create their new bike decals. We worked with them for a about a month on their new design and it more than payed off. The team at Racerman Racing was very satisfied with the quality and accuracy of our templates.

Goughary Racing

For our Professional project of the year in 2006, we did a body graphic for the Goughary Racing team. They were an uprising team back then and chose us to design and print their templates. With almost a month of man hours and 20 lbs of ink put into the boat, their decal turned out looking as professional as a veteran boat would look.
